When you need a quiet room for an undisturbed meeting, working or an important phone call Plenty Pod is the solution. Plenty Pod is a series of mobile, quiet rooms. They are especially suited for noisy areas such as open-plan offices and public spaces.

Complement your Plenty Pod with NEC video conferencing system – including a complete solution of monitor screen, speakers, and camcorder .

Complement your Plenty Pod with NEC video conferencing system – including a complete solution of monitor screen, speakers, and camcorder .

Plenty Pod quiet rooms not only offers silence on the inside.

It is also a shield, that dampens sound on the outside.

  • Choose between silver anodised, white anodised or painted in any RAL Classic colour on the aluminium frame.

  • Ventilation, LED lighting and electrical sockets come as standard.

  • Add fabric upholstered outer panels for better sound absorbing features, and a softer expression!

Plenty Pod is available in three different sizes, that can be customized after your specific needs and wishes.

Choose between Plenty Pod “Small”, “Medium” or “Large” quiet room module.

Make phone calls in lobbies and waiting rooms, without disturbing others.

Privacy and quietness make us more creative, motivated and focused. For that reason, we have developed Plenty Pod.

Technical specification

    (wxhxd mm)

    1200x2315x1200

    For one person, to be used as a phone booth.
    Also available as a module without interior floor.


  • “Medium”
    (wxhxd mm)

    2215x2315x1200

    For one or two persons, to be used as a quiet work station or a conversation room.

    Also available as a module without interior floor.


  • “Large”
    (wxhxd mm)

    2215x2315x2215

    For multiple persons, to be used as a meeting room or an office with work stations.
    Also available as a module without interior floor.
